Institute App

Teacher:
	- choose subjects
	- add subject content
	- see subject content
	- update subject content
	- delete subject content
	- give answers of questions
	- see answer of question
	- modify answer of question
	- see points earned
	- see the views and like to answer

Student: 
	- see subject content
	- ask question of subject(to every teacher for subject)
	- delete question
	- see answer for question.
	- see subject questions and answers given by each teacher
	- view answer
	- like the answer
